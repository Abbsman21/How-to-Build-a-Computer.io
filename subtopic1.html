 Purpose of a Computer

Abstract
 This site address what a general-purpose computer is which is a device capable of executing a wide range of tasks and applications, unlike computers made for one thing. This site goes over key components of the inside of a computer and what it is made up of such as the central processing unit/CPU, Memory/RAM, Input/Output, storage, and other upgradable aspects. These computers can be found basically anywhere such as your home, your school, your workplace, and even in your pockets, this general-purpose computer provides its users with communication, productivity, and entertainment. This type of computer is also flexible in the sense that you can easily build it and incorporate new and better parts to make your system run and perform amazingly. Operating systems are always included and there are several types including, MicrosoftWindows, Linux, and Chrome OS. You can even code and do software development by using programming languages such as Python and Java. These computers are gateways into creativity and make life easier for you. event.
